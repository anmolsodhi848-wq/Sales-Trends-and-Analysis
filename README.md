## Process

1. **Data Extraction & Cleaning:** Imported the raw sales dataset from MS Excel, removed duplicates, standardized date formats, corrected missing values, and ensured consistency across fields such as product categories, regions, and payment methods.
   
2. **Feature Engineering & Transformation:** Derived analytical fields such as Total Sales, Profit, Gross Margin, Discount Applied, Shipping Expense, and Average Order Value. Categorized data by month, quarter, region, and customer segments for deeper insights.

3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Performed trend analysis across sales, customer segments, product categories, and regions. Identified patterns in profit contribution, discount impact, sales channel efficiency, and seasonal variations.

4. **Dashboard Development & KPI Mapping**:Designed interactive dashboards to visualize KPIs, sales trends, regional performance, product ratings, and customer behavior. Mapped KPIs to business outcomes for quick decision-making.

## Project Insights

1. Sales & Profit Performance
   1. Total Sales reached 814M+, with a strong Gross Margin of 21.34%, indicating healthy profitability across product lines.
   2. Monthly sales remained consistent, with only slight fluctuations, showing stable customer demand throughout the year.
   3. Total Profit recorded is 173M+, with March, May, and October emerging as the highest-performing profit months.
   4. Average Order Value (AOV) is 81,961, reflecting strong customer purchase behavior and potential high-ticket products.

2. Customer Segment Trends
   1. High-Value, Low-Value, and Mid-Value customers contribute almost equally, showing a balanced customer distribution.
   2. Retail Stores lead overall sales, followed by Offline and Online channels, signaling the importance of in-store consumer engagement.
   3. Revenue consistency across segments suggests robust multi-channel performance.

3. Payment Method Insights
   1. All payment modes are used almost equally (approx. 2.4kâ€“2.5k transactions each).
   2. This indicates no dependency on a single payment channel, reducing operational risk and enhancing customer convenience.
   3. UPI and Debit Card show slightly higher counts, reflecting digital adoption and cash-less preference.

4. Customer Satisfaction Trends
   1. Higher product ratings correlate with higher total sales, confirming that customer satisfaction directly drives revenue.
   2. Most ratings cluster around 3.0, indicating moderate satisfactionâ€”an opportunity for product or service improvement.
   3. Products with slightly lower ratings show visibly reduced sales volume, highlighting the impact of perceived quality.

5. Weekly Purchase Patterns
   1. Sunday records the highest sales, followed by Wednesday and Friday, suggesting strong weekend and mid-week shopping behavior.
   2. Monday and Saturday show slightly lower sales, indicating potential areas for targeted promotions.
   3. Overall weekly sales remain stable, proving consistent customer engagement across the week.


## Conclusions

1. The business demonstrates strong overall performance, with over 814M in sales and a healthy profit margin of 21.34%.
2. Retail Stores, Offline, and Online segments contribute evenly, ensuring a well-balanced revenue stream across channels.
3. Customer satisfaction has a direct impact on sales, as higher product ratings are associated with higher revenue.
4. Weekly trends show Sunday, Wednesday, and Friday as peak sales days, indicating consistent and predictable buying behavior.
